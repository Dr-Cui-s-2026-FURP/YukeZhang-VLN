## Main Takeaways

- HAMT focuses on one of the central weaknesses of earlier VLN models: poor long-horizon memory.
- Its main idea is to replace compressed recurrent memory with explicit history modeling over panoramic observations and actions.
- The hierarchical transformer design is important because it makes long history processing more efficient.
- The paper is a strong bridge between benchmark-level VLN modeling and more realistic long-horizon navigation reasoning.
